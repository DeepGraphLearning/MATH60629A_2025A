Postdoctoral Fellow

Polytechnique Montréal

Golnoosh Farnadi obtained a Ph.D. in Computer science from KU Leuven and Ghent University in 2017 with a dissertation on user modeling in social media. She is currently a post-doctoral fellow at Polytechnique Montréal working on fairness-aware sequential decision making under uncertainty. Prior to this, she was a postdoc researcher of the Statistical Relational Learning Group (LINQS) of Prof. Lise Getoor at the University of California, Santa Cruz. She was a visiting scholar at UCLA, University of Washington, Tacoma, Tsinguha University, and Microsoft research, Redmond.
